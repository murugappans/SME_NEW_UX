How to assign claim supervisor to multiple employees?

Step 1: 
Click Manage Claim Supervisor Assignment under the Manage Assignment



Select the Claim Supervisors name




Step 2: 
Select the employee names from the left grid and Click on Assign button to assign the Claims Supervisor for the selected employees, for this senario Anita David.
