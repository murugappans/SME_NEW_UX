What image formats are supported for employee photo?

Smepayroll allows uploading employee's photo to the profile. The format supported are:

1. jpeg
2. gif
3. 

To upload employee's photo go to personal Tab under employee.  Click on the + icon below the default photo, browse for the location
of the photo and click save on the top of the page to upload the picture. Once successfully uploaded the photo will be displayed
everytime you visit the employee profile.

If no picture is upload the default photo as shown below will be available in the system.
